I observed that after completing the JavaScript Kata activities, the solutions frequently appeared straightforward. These tiny victories served as encouragement to complete the remainder of each workout. Therefore, the key lesson I learned was that when I feel stuck, it's generally a good idea to break the issue down further and focus on the tiny victories.
